Smart Parking System

Description:
Our IoT team successfully developed a Smart Parking System using Arduino Uno. With six slots allocated for parking, our system effectively monitors occupancy in real-time. Leveraging ultrasonic sensors strategically placed at each slot, the system continuously measures distances to detect the presence of vehicles. If all slots are occupied, indicating full capacity, the gate remains closed to prevent entry.

Required Components: Arduino Uno, Servo Motor, 1-Bread Board, 6 IR Sensors, Jumper Wires & Powerbank
